I attended the 2025 Workshop of the BK21 Graduate program in System Health Science and Engineering.
The workshop is organized to celebrate the achievements of this year, promote research exchange, and strengthen collaboration among faculty members and participating students.

During the event, outstanding research contributions were recognized, and I was honored to receive the Outstanding Academic Research Award.
The workshop provided a valuable opportunity to reflect on my research progress and to engage in meaningful discussions for future academic and program development.
